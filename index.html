Overview

Echoing the historic spirit of the 1955 Asian–African Conference (KAA) in Bandung, the Saudi–Indonesia Youth Meeting 2026 emerges as a bilateral movement designed to transform youth diplomacy into a lasting legacy of global solidarity. By drawing on the vision of Mandeh Haramain and the strategic partnership between PPMI Riyadh and the LOGIC Initiative. This gathering reclaims the Bandung Spirit not merely as a historical memory, but as a living blueprint for youth-led global solidarity, fostering a modern era of visionary leadership and sustainable future-building. Strategically aligned with the Human Capability Development Program under Saudi Vision 2030, this initiative serves as a catalyst in the Kingdom's ambitious journey to empower citizens and foster global citizenship. It reinterprets the 1955 call for Afro-Asian cooperation through cross-cultural synergy and a redefined approach to education that transcends traditional boundaries.

By positioning volunteering as a vital instrument for character building and soft diplomacy, the meeting provides a platform for young leaders to cultivate the wisdom and leadership necessary to contribute meaningfully to the progress of humanity. It aims to elevate youth initiatives from traditional community service into professionally managed, sustainable projects—transforming the Bandung spirit into social innovation and architecting a collaborative roadmap that directly aligns the aspirations of Saudi Vision 2030 with the Indonesia Golden Vision 2045.

Our Event Aims To

Professionalize Service

To transform youthful energy into structured social impact. By transitioning from traditional charity to purposeful, managed action, we ensure that volunteer initiatives achieve the same rigor and effectiveness as professional global projects.

Cultivate 'Diplomat-Managers'

Nurturing leaders who possess the cultural empathy of a diplomat and the operational precision of a manager—individuals equipped to build international bridges while executing vision with excellence.

Education through Service

Positioning volunteering as a living laboratory for leadership development beyond the classroom. Advocating that the most profound learning occurs through action.

Architect a Shared Future

Strategically aligning Saudi Vision 2030 and Indonesia Golden Vision 2045. Making Indonesian and Saudi youth active stakeholders in driving a prosperous and socially conscious future.

I. Context

Background & Rationale

The bilateral relationship has evolved into a strategic partnership. Within this landscape, youth and diaspora communities have emerged as "Diplomat-Managers"—non-state actors who combine diplomatic vision with technical management skills.

X. Expected Outputs

  • Shared Networking & Rapport: Establishing a strong personal and professional connection between Indonesian students (PPMI Riyadh) and Saudi youth (LOGIC Initiative) as the foundational step for long-term collaboration.
  • Introduction to Professional Standards: Providing participants with a practical overview of how social passion can be enhanced through professional management, learning from LOGIC's structured approach.
  • Foundation for Future Action: Securing an initial agreement (Statement of Intent) to begin drafting a single, joint community initiative in Riyadh, marking the official start of the partnership between our two communities.
