Overview

On the significant occasion of Indonesian National Education Day (Hardiknas), the Saudi–Indonesia Youth Meeting 2026 emerges as a bilateral movement designed to transform youth diplomacy into a lasting legacy of social impact. Drawing on the vision of Mandeh Haramain and the strategic partnership between PPMI Riyadh and the LOGIC Initiative, this inaugural milestone serves as a platform where diplomatic friendship meets the professional craft of project management.

By redefining education beyond the classroom, the meeting positions volunteering as a vital instrument for character building and soft diplomacy, aiming to elevate youth initiatives from traditional community service into professionally managed, sustainable projects. Ultimately, this synergy empowers the spirit of charity into social innovation, architecting a collaborative roadmap that directly aligns the aspirations of Saudi Vision 2030 with Indonesia Golden Vision 2045.

Our Event Aims To

Professionalize Service

To transform youthful energy into structured social impact. By transitioning from traditional charity to purposeful, managed action, we ensure that volunteer initiatives achieve the same rigor and effectiveness as professional global projects.

Cultivate ‘Diplomat-Managers’

Nurturing leaders who possess the cultural empathy of a diplomat and the operational precision of a manager—individuals equipped to build international bridges while executing vision with excellence.

Education through Service

Positioning volunteering as a living laboratory for leadership development beyond the classroom. Advocating that the most profound learning occurs through action.

Architect a Shared Future

Strategically aligning Saudi Vision 2030 and Indonesia Golden Vision 2045. Making Indonesian and Saudi youth active stakeholders in driving a prosperous and socially conscious future.

X. Expected Outputs

  • Shared Networking & Rapport: Establishing a strong personal and professional connection between Indonesian students (PPMI Riyadh) and Saudi youth (LOGIC Initiative) as the foundational step for long-term collaboration.
  • Introduction to Professional Standards: Providing participants with a practical overview of how social passion can be enhanced through professional management, learning from LOGIC’s structured approach.
  • Foundation for Future Action: Securing an initial agreement (Statement of Intent) to begin drafting a single, joint community initiative in Riyadh, marking the official start of the partnership between our two communities.
